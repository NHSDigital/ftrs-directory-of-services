## Description

Operational alignment to published engineering standards with tracked exceptions.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. GIVEN a standards baseline WHEN published THEN the team checklist is updated within two weeks
2. GIVEN a deviation WHEN identified THEN an exception with a remediation target date is logged
3. GIVEN a quarterly audit WHEN performed THEN a compliance scorecard is produced

## Out of Scope

- Creation of a new enterprise-wide standards body

## Implementation Notes

- Evidence: baseline document, exception log, audit scorecard

## Summary

Operational alignment to published engineering standards with tracked exceptions.
